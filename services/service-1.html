About service

Our Approach
and Capabilities

We specialize in engineering complex mechanical and aerospace structures for fixed-wing aircraft, multicopters, hybrid VTOLs, helicopters, and other autonomous platforms. Our expertise covers electric and fuel combustion propulsion systems, structural design, aerodynamics, and full systems integration, delivering high-performance and reliable technologies.

View Projects

Structural Design

+
-

We develop robust mechanical structures for UAVs, VTOLs, and helicopters optimized for strength, weight, and stability.

Our designs are suitable for both electric and fuel-based propulsion systems and tailored to specific mission requirements.

Aerodynamic Optimization

+
-

We analyze lift, drag, and stability characteristics for fixed-wing, multicopter, and hybrid VTOL platforms.

Simulations ensure efficiency, endurance, and safe operation in real-world conditions.

Propulsion & Systems Integration

+
-

We integrate electric motors, combustion engines, and hybrid propulsion with avionics and control systems.

This ensures seamless interaction between mechanical, electrical, and software components for reliable performance.

Prototyping & Testing

+
-

We build and test prototypes to validate structural integrity, aerodynamics, and system functionality.

Iterative testing ensures performance, safety, and compliance with industry standards.
