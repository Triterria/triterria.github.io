Indigenous Avionics & Power Electronics

Read more
Client:  Boltz R&D
Date:  August 2025
Credits  Miklos Gal (USA)

Engineering Reliable Avionics
and Intelligent Power Systems

Here we showcase our indigenous design and development capabilities in avionics and power electronics hardware for robotics and aerial platforms. Our team has engineered advanced control, communication, and power management systems purpose-built for drones, autonomous vehicles, and industrial applications.

Developed entirely in-house, the product range includes drone avionics, custom flight controllers, power distribution units, and embedded boards for AI edge computing such as NVIDIA Jetson carriers. These systems are optimized for performance, efficiency, and modular scalability.

We’ve also created hardware platforms for 3D printers, stepper driver standards, and precision motion controllers—bridging innovation between robotics, additive manufacturing, and aerospace systems.

Our focus remains on designing dependable, production-ready electronic systems that enable the next generation of intelligent, autonomous, and connected technologies.
