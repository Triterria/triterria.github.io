About Us

Autonomy, Robotics, Embedded Firmware, Hardware and Deep Tech Consultants

We are a team of engineers, technologists, and innovators driven by a shared passion for building intelligent, high-performance systems. With over eight years of hands-on experience in developing mechanical systems, electronics, firmware, and advanced software across robotics, UAVs, UGVs, AI, and embedded platforms, we collaborate to create end-to-end solutions for complex, real-world challenges.


Our founding members bring deep expertise in indigenous hardware and system design — including custom flight controllers, high-power electronic speed controllers (ESCs), BLDC motor architectures, and power distribution systems integrated with Jetson-based compute modules. The team has extensive experience in designing ground control systems, advanced avionics, and aerostructures for fixed-wing, multirotor, and hybrid VTOL aircraft.


We’ve contributed to India’s first DGCA Type Certification program and pioneered advancements in swarm coordination, real-time autonomy, and collision avoidance using computer vision, machine learning, and reinforcement learning. Beyond aerial platforms, our portfolio includes development of embedded AI frameworks, sensor fusion systems, high-efficiency power electronics, and rugged edge-compute devices for field robotics and industrial automation.


At our core, we are committed to engineering reliability, scalability, and intelligence into every system we build — empowering the next generation of autonomous and connected technologies.
